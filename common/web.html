      Web accessibility

Web accessibility means that websites, tools, 
and technologies are designed and developed so 
that people with disabilities can use them

- Make Sure Your Site Is Keyboard-Friendly and easily accessible
- Add Alt Text to All Images
- Choose Your Colors Carefully (should be easy for color blindness)
- Design Your Forms for Accessibility
